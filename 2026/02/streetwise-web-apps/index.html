The Year Zero Engine is very flexible and I like the fact that it uses six sided dice, the most common size. However, once you get an attribute and a skill going with possibly a modifier or two you can rapidly end up rolling a lot of them! In our first play test, being able to roll lots of dice easily was a sticking point, especially given the Streetwise Scene Strain mechanic (rather like Stress in Alien RPG) where rolling Banes (1s) can add dice to future pools for everyone.

In the end, to make play testing more streamlined, I put together a quick dice roller based on the current state of the Streetwise Core Rules. It’s a work in progress but you can have a go at:

https://ronanchilvers.github.io/yze-roller/

In addition, having some way to rapidly manage characters (and possibly even generate random ones) is extremely useful. One of my most dedicated players spent some time developing a builder for Streetwise characters. You can have a go at:

https://urchinator.notoriety.co.uk/
